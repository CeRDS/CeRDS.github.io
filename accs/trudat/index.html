TruDat (MyTARDIS) data store

Data management for scientific instruments

TruDat is a branded deployment of MyTardis for the storage and pre-processing of instrumentation data. Located within Pawsey infrastructure the data is in close proximity to Cloud and HPC infrastructure as well as the Characterisation Virtual Laboratory (CVL) instance it cohabits with.
